Intro

As an imminent graduate with a Master of Science in Information Systems from Northeastern University, I am continuously expanding my expertise in data science, with a primary focus on data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and AI. Proficient in Python and SQL, my role as a Quantitative Research Intern at AlgoTrade involved conducting thorough business and data analysis, giving me insight into the significant impact data has on strategic decision-making in financial markets. I have engineered predictive models across various projects, utilizing a range of algorithms and solving complex problems. My ability to transform data into actionable insights through advanced statistical techniques has proven to drive informed decision-making.

With a passion for tackling challenging problems, I am eager to apply my skills to enhance corporate performance and adapt to the latest advancements in data science. Living by Yoda's words, I confront challenges head-on and strive for excellence in the dynamic data science field.

Outside of work, literature is a major passion of mine. I enjoy exploring different narratives and perspectives, sharing my thoughts on Goodreads. This enthusiasm for books complements my analytical mindset, allowing me to delve deep into stories and data alike, uncovering insights in both realms.
